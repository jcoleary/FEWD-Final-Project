With an overall area of 30.3 million km2 (11.7 mi2) and a population of 1.1 billion people, Africa is the world's second largest continent by poth area and population. 54 countries currently comprise Africa, inculding the world's youngest, South Sudan, which became a nation in 2011. Most countries in Africa were derived from the archaic boundaries of nineteenth-century European colonization. While Africa has demonstrated a considerable amount of rapid development since the 1950s, Africa still suffers from the legacies of colonization and exploitation.
